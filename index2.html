Image-based password authentication (IBPA) with AES encryption adds an extra layer of security to the login process. Instead of typing a password, you choose specific images to log in. These chosen images are then encrypted using the Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) before being stored on the system. During login, your selected images are compared against the encrypted versions stored securely.
